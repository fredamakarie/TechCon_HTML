    • History

    TechCon SoCal is a conference focused on technology and innovation in Southern California, specifically targeting investors, innovators, and entrepreneurs. It was founded by Faisal Mushtaq, whose experience includes scaling Fortune 500 companies and leading startups. The conference aims to connect visionaries, nurture entrepreneurship, and catalyze investments in the tech sector. TechCon SoCal is known for its multi-track format, high-caliber speakers, and focus on quality over quantity. It covers a wide range of topics, including AI, digital health, robotics, consumer tech, data, software, and semiconductors. The conference provides a platform for impactful discussions, strategic partnerships, and mentorship opportunities.

    Mission

    Our mission extends beyond financial support; we aim to inspire the next generation of entrepreneurs by: Striving together to build a brighter future where innovation thrives, investments abound, entrepreneurship flourishes, and positive change permeates every aspect of society. Providing mentorship, resources, and networking opportunities to help them realize their aspirations. Recognizing the importance of advocacy and collaboration in shaping policies and frameworks conducive to innovation and entrepreneurship. Engaging with policymakers, industry leaders, and other stakeholders to create an ecosystem that fosters innovation, rewards risk-taking, and promotes inclusive growth.
